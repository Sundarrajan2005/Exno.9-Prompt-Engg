## Aim:

To explore how different prompting techniques influence the quality, style, and realism of AI-generated videos using modern text-to-video tools.

## Algorithm / Procedure:

Choose any AI video-generation platforms such as:

* Runway Gen-2

Start with a simple base prompt and generate an initial video.

## Apply different prompting techniques such as:

* Detail Enhancement

* Cinematic Prompting

* Artistic / Stylized Prompting

* Regenerate the video for each refined prompt.

## Compare the visual differences such as:

* Motion

* Lighting

* Artistic style

* Realism

* Scene composition

## Document outputs and observations.
~~~
Base Prompt

"Ocean waves moving gently under the morning sunlight with seagulls flying above."
~~~
## Prompt Refinement Types

## 1. Detailed Prompt:

"Crystal-clear ocean waves rolling gently under warm morning sunlight, with white seagulls gliding across the bright blue sky and soft reflections shimmering on the water surface."


## Expected Output:
A detailed, nature-realistic ocean scene with smooth wave motion and bright lighting.

## 2. Cinematic Prompt:

"A cinematic wide-angle shot of ocean waves moving under golden sunrise light, captured with slow camera zoom. Seagulls fly across the horizon while soft lens flare enhances the scene’s dramatic tone." 

## Expected Output:

Movie-like visuals, dynamic camera movement, warm cinematic color grading.

## 3. Artistic Prompt:

"A watercolor-style animated video of calm ocean waves under a pastel sky, with hand-drawn seagulls flying gently above. Soft brush textures and artistic color blending."

## Expected Output:
A stylized, animated ocean scene with painted textures and smooth artistic motion.

“A cinematic slow-motion shot of waves crashing gently onto a golden sandy beach during sunset. The sky glows with warm orange, purple, and pink tones, with reflections shimmering on the surface of the water. Soft wind moves through small coastal plants in the foreground. The camera performs a slow dolly-in movement toward the shoreline, capturing the motion of the waves in ultra-realistic 4K detail. High dynamic range lighting, soft warm tones, peaceful and atmospheric mood, cinematic depth of field, natural ocean ambience.”
